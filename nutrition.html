Nutrition & Recovery Tips

Good nutrition and recovery are just as important as your workouts. Here are some simple, effective tips to fuel your body and stay strong.

Get 7–9 hours of sleep every night to help your muscles recover.

Avoid junk food and sugary drinks—they slow down your progress.

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated.

Eat healthy snacks like yogurt, bananas, or eggs before working out.

Include fruits, protein, and healthy carbs in your daily meals.

Eat carbs before and protein after workouts to boost performance.
