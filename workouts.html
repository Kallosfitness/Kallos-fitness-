Calisthenics Workout Plan

This plan focuses on five key exercises to build strength, control, and endurance using just your bodyweight.

Beginner

  • Push-ups – 3 sets of 10
  • Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets of 15
  • Chair Dips – 3 sets of 8
  • Negative Pull-ups – 3 sets of 5
  • Incline Push-ups – 3 sets of 12

Intermediate

  • Push-ups – 4 sets of 15
  • Air Squats – 4 sets of 20
  • Full Dips – 3 sets of 10
  • Pull-ups – 3 sets of 6–8
  • Assisted Muscle-ups – 3 sets of 3

Advanced

  • Push-ups – 5 sets of 20 (clap/archer)
  • Pistol Squats – 3 sets of 6 per leg
  • Dips – 4 sets of 12 (weighted)
  • Pull-ups – 4 sets of 10 (wide grip)
  • Muscle-ups – 3 sets of 3–5
